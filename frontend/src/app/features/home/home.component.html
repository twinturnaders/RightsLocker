Laws & Guidance · How RightsLocker works

Workflow

How it works

  1. Add your file. Upload a photo or video for processing.
  2. Generate a review package. RightsLocker extracts metadata, computes hashes, and prepares the package.
  3. Export and share. Download the processed file, metadata PDF, and supporting integrity details.

Integrity

We compute SHA-256 for the original and derivative assets so exports can be validated later.

Chain of custody

RightsLocker records timestamps and processing events to support a defensible review trail.

Transparency

Each package includes metadata and reporting artifacts that are easy to review and export.

Processing only

Upload, process, and download. Files are removed automatically unless legal hold is enabled.

Hosted locker

Authenticated users can retain evidence, enable legal hold, and work from the evidence dashboard.
