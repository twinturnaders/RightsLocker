How it works

  1. Add your files – drag & drop or browse.
  2. Choose protections – metadata stripping and redaction tools.
  3. Download your package – originals + derivatives + printable report (hashes, steps, timestamps).

Integrity

We compute SHA-256 for the original and each derivative. Matching hashes = unchanged content.

Chain of custody

We log timestamps, versions, and actions to create a tamper-evident activity trail.

Transparency

Download a printable report to share with counsel, investigators, or the court.

Processing-only (default)

Upload → process → download. We auto-delete within 24 hours.

Hosted Locker (opt-in)

We retain files per your retention policy. You can enable legal hold and share links.
