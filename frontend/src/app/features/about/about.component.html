About RightsLocker

RightsLocker helps you preserve integrity and privacy of photo/video evidence. We compute cryptographic hashes, log custody events, and create redacted derivatives for safe sharing.

Processing-only (default)

Upload, process, download a verifiable package. We auto-delete within 24 hours.

Hosted Locker (opt-in)

We retain originals + derivatives per your retention policy. You can enable legal hold and share links. You should still keep your own copy.

Integrity
SHA-256 digests of original and delivered files
Privacy
Automatic face-blur (toggleable) with conservative defaults
Transparency
Printable report with timestamps, versions, and hashes
