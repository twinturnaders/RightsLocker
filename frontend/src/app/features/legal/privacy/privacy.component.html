Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 11, 2025

Last updated: November 11, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how RightsLocker ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our services. We process data to provide evidence packaging, hashing, chain-of-custody, reporting, optional hosting, and abuse prevention.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email, password hash, organization, settings.
  • Content: files you upload and the derivatives we generate (thumbnails, redacted copies, reports).
  • Metadata & logs: filenames, sizes, hashes (SHA-256), timestamps, processing actions, IP addresses, device/browser info, and audit logs.
  • Payments (if applicable): last 4 digits, transaction IDs, and billing details (handled by our payment processor).
  • Support/abuse reports: messages, attachments, and related diagnostic data.

3. How we use information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the service;
  • Generate hashes, chain-of-custody logs, and reports;
  • Optional hosting and legal hold;
  • Prevent abuse and enforce Terms;
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., preservation, lawful disclosures, mandatory reporting);
  • Communicate about updates, security, and support.

4. Legal bases (if applicable outside the U.S.)

Where required (e.g., GDPR jurisdictions), we rely on performance of a contract, legitimate interests in providing a secure service, compliance with legal obligations, and your consent (when needed).

5. Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Service providers (infrastructure, storage, analytics, email, payment) bound by confidentiality;
  • Law enforcement/authorities when required by law or to protect safety, including mandatory CSAM reporting to NCMEC;
  • Your organization/recipients when you share links or reports.

We do not sell personal data.

6. Retention

  • Processing-only: files auto-delete within 24 hours after package generation (unless you enable legal hold or we receive a legal preservation request).
  • Hosted Locker: we retain per your settings; Legal Hold suspends deletion.
  • We may retain logs/hashes/reports as necessary for security, integrity, and compliance.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (access controls, encryption in transit/at rest where applicable, least-privilege admin access). No system is 100% secure.

8. Children

The service is not for children under 13. We prohibit CSAM and will report apparent CSAM to NCMEC when we become aware.

9. Your choices

  • Access, download, or delete your content (subject to legal holds/preservation).
  • Configure retention and holds in Hosted Locker mode.
  • Opt-out of non-essential emails.

10. International transfers

If data is transferred across borders, we use appropriate safeguards where required by law.

11. Changes

We may update this policy; we'll post the new effective date. Continued use means acceptance.
